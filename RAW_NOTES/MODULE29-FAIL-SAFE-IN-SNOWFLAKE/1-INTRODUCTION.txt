"fail-safe provides a (non-configurable) 7-DAY PERIOD 
DURING WHICH HISTORICAL DATA IS RECOVERABLE BY SNOWFLAKE. THIS PERIOD 
STARTS IMMEDIATELY AFTER THE TIME TRAVEL RETENTION PERIOD ENDS"...

--> MAS PQ DEVEMOS USAR O FAIL SAFE?








"""DATA CORRUPTION OR LOSS CAN HAPPEN WITH ANY DATABASE 
MANAGEMENT SYSTEM. TO MITIGATE THE RISK,

DBAs TRADITIONALLY PERFORM FULL AND INCREMENTAL BACKUPS, BUT 

THIS CAN DOUBLE OR EVEN __tRIPLE__ OVERALL DATA STORAGE. 

IN ADDITION, DATA RECOVERY CAN BE PAINFUL AND COSTLY DUE TO NUMEROUS 
FACTORS, INCLUDING:""


1) TIME REQUIRED TO RELOAD LOST DATA 


2) BUSINESS DOWNTIME DURING RECOVERY 


3) LOSS OF DATA SINCE THE LAST BACKUP...


















-> SE VC HABILITA O FAILSAFE,



O SNOWFLAKE 

VAI CUIDAR DOS BACKUPS DAS SUAS TABLES... É UM CONTINUOUS 

BACKUP FEATURE,

PROVIDENCIADO PELO SNOWFLAKE....
